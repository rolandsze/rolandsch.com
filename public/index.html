About Me

I'm Roland Szecsenyi, a 26 years old full stack web developer from Hungary. I've always been interested in the wonder of programming and wanted to be able to create since I'm young. Hence I stepped onto the road of self teaching around 2004.

Nowadays I am more into front-end development even though PHP have always been my favorite language. At days, I bring life into web sites and applications for companies; at nights, I like to surf the web for articles, documentaries and videos to educate myself.
